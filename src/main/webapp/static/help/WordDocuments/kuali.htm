Kuali

“Kuali” is an umbrella of applications development, under which smaller applications known as modules are developed to support coarse-grained end-user tasks. Modules are portions of larger programs that carry out specific functions. They can be implemented alone or can be combined with other modules.

KRA’s modular architecture allows institutions to implement only the functional elements they need. In this way, KRA can be scaled to meet the needs of institutions of any size.
