Contact Type

Use the Contact Type screen to look up, create, and update Contact Type Codes.  Contact Type Codes identify points of contact for different areas of responsibility on an award.  For example, Contact Type Codes provide a way to distinguish between administrative, technical, financial, and reporting contacts on a single award. On the Contacts tab of the KC Award e-doc, you select a Contact Type from the Project Role list from the Unit Contacts, Sponsor Contacts and Central Administration Contacts sub-panels in the Unit Contacts panel.
