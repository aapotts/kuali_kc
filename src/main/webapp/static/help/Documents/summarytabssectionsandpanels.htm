Summary: Tabs, Sections, and Panels

•      Tabs are like pages in an e-doc.  Click a tab to display its contents.  The tab that KC is displaying is a slightly darker gray than the other e-doc tabs.

•      Panels and sub-panels are parts of a tab.  You can expand and collapse then using the  -  function.

•      Sections on a tab, panel, or sub-panel do not open or close.
