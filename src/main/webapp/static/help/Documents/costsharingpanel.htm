Cost Sharing Panel

This panel has two sections: The Cost Sharing Distribution List section displays fields to allocate cost sharing committed in the budget to the appropriate source (account or department).  The Cost Sharing Summary section displays cost sharing totals by the project period. 

Figure 255 Cost Sharing Panel

Cost sharing totals populate from the line items added to the proposal budget, and include both Personnel and Non-Personnel expenses.  To add additional cost sharing amounts to the totals on the Distribution and Income, you must add cost shared expenses to the budget pages.

You must add a line item for each account, if more than one source account provides cost sharing support during a single fiscal year or budget period.  A summary of the cost sharing line items is displayed by project period at the bottom of the panel.

Cost Sharing Distribution List section

Fiscal Year

Defaults to the institution’s fiscal year in which the cost share expense occurs. The user may enter this as the project year, or subdivision of a project year, if appropriate.

Percentage

The proportion of total cost sharing to be allocated for the fiscal year/project period and Source Account listed for the line item

Enter the percentage as a whole number, not a calculated decimal.

Example: Enter 10.35 for 10.35%.  Do not enter 0.1035 for 10.35%  This field does not perform any calculations.

Source Account

The account number or description of the funding source for the allocated cost share amount. 

This field accepts text and numbers; It is alphanumeric.

Amount

The dollar amount of budgeted cost share that is assignable to each fiscal year and funding source.
