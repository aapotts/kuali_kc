Contacts Tab

Use the four panels in the Contacts tab to add, track, and identify the people associated with the project, link them to the Award, and associate them with a project role.  The Contacts tab links all contacts (individuals and organizations) to the award and associates each contact with a project role.  The Contacts tab allows users to create, maintain, and retrieve a list of persons involved on the award for effective award administration. 

Click  on a panel to display its information.
