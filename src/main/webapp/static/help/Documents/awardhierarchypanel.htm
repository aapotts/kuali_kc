Award Hierarchy Panel

The Award Hierarchy panel provides a structured, graphical representation of a sponsored project in a tree view.

There are many ways to implement the award hierarchy.  Your institution decides how to implement its award hierarchy, if they choose to use an award hierarchy.

Click  on the Award Hierarchy panel to display its information:
