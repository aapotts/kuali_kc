Adding a Budget Period

•      Go to the top row of the Budget Periods section.

•      In the Period Start Date column, click  to select a start date for the new budget period.

•      In the Period End Date column, click  to select an end date for the new budget period.

•      You may choose to enter data in the fields to the right of the Period End Date.

•      On the far right of the Add row, click  to insert the new budget period.

It is not necessary to enter data in the other columns of the new budget period before adding it to your budget version.
