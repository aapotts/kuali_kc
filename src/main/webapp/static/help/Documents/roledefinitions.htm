Role Definitions

KC defines these roles:

•      Principal Investigator

•      Co-Investigator

•      Key Person

NIH applications define the Principal Investigator as the PI/Contact and the Co-Investigator as the PI/Multiple.  KC automatically displays the NIH role definition for NIH proposals. 

In this documentation, the Principal Investigator or PI/Contact may be referred to as the PI.  The Co-Investigator and PI/Multiple may be referred to as Co-I.
