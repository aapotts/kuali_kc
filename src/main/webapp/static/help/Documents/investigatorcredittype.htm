Investigator Credit Type

Use the Investigator Credit Type maintenance document to lookup, create, and update Investigator Credit Types.  Many schools use credit types to capture the distribution or allocation of F&A revenue, academic credit, space, or other items. Investigator Credit Split may appear in several KC e-docs, or may not appear at all, depending on your institution’s implementation.
