Keywords Panel

The Keywords panel is a common e-doc panel.  It contains keywords available for association with a proposal.  The Keywords panel is optional.  Your institution may not use the Keywords panel.

Keywords become associated with a specific proposal through tasks you perform on the Keywords panel.  Once associated with a proposal, the keywords help you locate the proposal when doing research or preparing reports.  Two examples of keywords are dermatology and metabolism.  Your institution may have its own keyword list or use the KC default keywords.

Your institution defines keywords in KC Maintenance e-docs.  Contact your KC system administrator for more information about keywords.
