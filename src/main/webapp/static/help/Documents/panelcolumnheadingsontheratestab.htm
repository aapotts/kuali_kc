Institute Rate

 

Read-only

 

A percentage of budgeted direct costs as determined by the institution’s negotiated Federal rate agreement for both F & A costs and fringe benefit rates

The base rate is the direct costs less exclusions when referring to F & A cost calculations. 

The Institute Rate is the default rate for budget calculations unless an Applicable Rate exists.

* Applicable Rate

Numerical Field

 

Rates entered in this field override the Institute Rate on the same line. 

Use Applicable Rates on a proposal when you need a rate other than the Institute Rate to calculate F & A costs or fringe benefits.  For example, some sponsors require specific F & A or fringe benefit rates.  These sponsor-required rates may be lower than your institution’s negotiated rates.

Action Buttons

Click  to synchronize all Institute Rates and Applicable Rates on the panel with the current Institute Rates in the Unit Hierarchy rate table. 

This action overrides the existing rates in both columns and causes KC to recalculate the budget.

Click  to undo changes made to the Applicable Rate fields on the panel and reset them to match existing Institute Rates. 

This action deletes manually entered Applicable Rates and recalculates the budget.

•        

When you begin work on a new budget, click  to ensure your displayed rates are up-to-date.  This is particularly important when the current proposal is a copy of an earlier proposal that includes old rates.

 

KC applies all applicable rates to line item costs you enter through panels on the Personnel tab and Non-Personnel tab.  Calculations involving rates take place automatically, based on the Object Code Name (also known as a cost element), the proposal Activity Type, and the On Campus flag.
