Distribution & Income Tab

The Distribution & Income tab is used to allocate any cost sharing committed in the budget, including direct and indirect (F&A costs).  This tab provides data entry fields for details related to Cost Sharing, Unrecovered F&A costs, and Project Income (see Figure 254).  The first two panels provide a way to show the source for cost sharing amounts and unrecovered F&A costs.  If your project is expected to generate income via activities funded by the sponsor, record the anticipated income in the Project Income panel.
