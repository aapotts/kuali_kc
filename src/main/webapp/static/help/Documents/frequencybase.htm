Frequency Base

Use the Frequency Base screen to lookup, create, and update Frequency Base Codes.  Frequency Base Codes identify the date used by KC in calculating the due date of required reports submitted on a specific frequency.

Required reports submitted on a specific frequency base have an associated Frequency Base Code.  Award terms and conditions may require the submission of technical, financial, patent, and other reports on a scheduled basis or a specific frequency.

For example, if an award requires your institution to submit quarterly financial reports, KC calculates the deadline for each quarterly report from the pre-defined date.  Pre-defined dates may include the award’s execution date, the award’s effective date, its final expiration date, or another date added as a Frequency Base Code.
