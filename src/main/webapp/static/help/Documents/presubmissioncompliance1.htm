Pre-Submission Compliance

The Pre-Submission Compliance group on the Unit menu provides convenient, efficient access to KC functions that are commonly used by Unit users.  It contains “create new” and “lookup existing” icon buttons that take you to new, blank e-docs or lookup screens that allow you to search for e-docs, respectively.

Figure 85 Unit Menu – Pre-Submission Compliance Group
