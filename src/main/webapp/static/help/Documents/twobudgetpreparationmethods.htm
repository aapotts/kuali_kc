Two Budget Preparation Methods

Creating a budget version is the first step in preparing a budget. 

When you prepare a budget version, you may create either a summary budget or a detailed budget:

1.  Summary budget:

Prepare a summary budget using the Budget Periods and Totals panel found on the Parameters tab.  If you enter budget information on the Personnel tab and/or the Non-Personnel tab, it replaces information entered on the Summary tab.

2.  Detailed budget: 

Prepare a detailed budget using the Personnel, Non-personnel, Distribution & Income, and Modular Budget tabs.  Information entered on these tabs replaces information entered on the Summary tab.

Detailed budget information replaces information entered on the Summary tab.  However, a summary budget cannot update a detailed budget.
