Logging In

Arriving at the URL of your KC implementation, you will see the default screen with five Main Menu Tabs near the top.  When first entering KC, you are not yet logged in.  After selecting a tab or button, you are prompted to login.  After logging in, you are directed to the screen you sought by selecting the tab or button that started the login cycle.

If your institution opts to integrate the user authentication to a different campus authentication system, the login sequence might vary.

Enter your User ID and Password and click Login.
