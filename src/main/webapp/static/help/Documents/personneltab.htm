Personnel Tab

Use the Personnel tab to add or delete project personnel from the project proposal.  You may add specific people found in either the employee or the non-employee lookups.  If you do not know who is to fill a position, you can create a To-be-named placeholder for a position or role. 

Figure 237 Panels on the Personnel Tab

 

Adding or deleting entries on the Project Personnel panel has no effect on the contents of the Key Personnel tab on the Proposal Development Document.  If you enter a name on the Key Personnel tab, you must also delete it there. 

 

You cannot delete a name if expenses for that person remain on the Personnel Detail panel.  You may delete To-be-named positions and Non-employee lines, but not Employee lines unless you first delete the employee's budget details.
