Unrecovered F&A Panel

The Unrecovered F&A panel is where you specify F&A costs which are not requested or funded by the sponsor.

Figure 256 Unrecovered F & A Panel

 

Facilities and Administrative Costs are real costs incurred by an institution in support of sponsored activities, but cannot be easily identified with a specific project or sponsored activity.  The costs result from shared services or are incurred to benefit common and joint objectives.  F&A costs are synonymous with indirect costs, IDC, and overhead costs.

There are times when a sponsor (federal, foundation, industry, etc), will not pay the full F&A rate established by the institution.  When this occurs, a different F&A rate is applied to the cost base utilized by the project (Total Direct Cost, Modified Total Direct Cost, or other).  When a rate other than the institutional rate is applied to the project, the system generates the Unrecovered F&A, also known as under recovered costs.  In these cases, where full indirect costs are not recovered by sponsor provided funds, the institution funds the difference.

The system generates an unrecovered amount under the following circumstances:

1.  Unrecovered F&A on individual budget line items:  When an Apply Rate listed on the Rate Classes section of a panel on either the Personnel or Non–Personnel tab is unchecked, KC applies the entire Institute rate as Unrecovered F&A to the applicable line items. 

•         Unrecovered F & A amounts show as Unallocated.

2.  Unrecovered F&A on the entire budget:  When an Apply Rate listed on the Rate Classes section of a panel on either the Personnel or Non–Personnel tab is checked, but on the F & A panel of the Rates tab the Applicable Rate (manually entered) is lower than the Institute Rate, KC shows the difference as Unrecovered F&A.

3.  The same conditions apply to Cost Sharing amounts entered on the panels of the Personnel and Non–Personnel tabs.
