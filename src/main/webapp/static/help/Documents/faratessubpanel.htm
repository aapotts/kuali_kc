F&A Rates Sub-Panel

The F&A Rates sub-panel displays the facilities and administrative cost rate(s) that are associated with an award.  You may enter different rates for multiple periods, if appropriate.

The panel details information on:

•      Rate – F&A percentage and base for calculation

•      Time – The fiscal year(s), start and end dates associated with each rate

•      Place – On/off campus indicator

•      Unrecovered F&A – Dollar amount, expense destination, and revenue source to cover this amount

•      Comments – Details about the F&A rates

 

Click  on the F&A Rates sub-panel to display its information:
