Error Messages – Sponsor Hierarchy

These screen prints show errors you might encounter when creating and maintaining a Sponsor Hierarchy:

Figure 684 Error Message – Empty Group

You cannot save a Sponsor Hierarchy without at least one group and one sponsor.

 

Figure 685 Error Message – Has Sub Sponsor Group

Add sponsors only at the lowest level of a particular grouping.  You cannot have subgroups and sponsors at the same level in a Sponsor Hierarchy.
