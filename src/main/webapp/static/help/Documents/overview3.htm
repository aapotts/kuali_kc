Overview

Kuali Coeus (KC) is a community source software project charged with development of an integrated set of research administration services for higher education.  KC offers a comprehensive suite of modules including Proposal and Budget Development, Workflow, and Grants.gov integration.

A key component of KC is an electronic document (also known as “e-doc”) environment in which e-docs are initiated on a personal computer and electronically routed through an approval process.  KC is important to those who use and process research administration information for a variety of reasons.  KC reduces paper processes; enables faster turnaround and allows for decisions to be made based on up-to-date information; provides built-in checks and balances reducing mistakes and the need to correct errors; gives more control and management flexibility to documents; and facilitates efficient tracking, archiving and reporting.  KC Overview is intended to be a guide for the shared functionality and common operations of most e-docs as well as a foundation for subsequent topics covering specific functionality.
