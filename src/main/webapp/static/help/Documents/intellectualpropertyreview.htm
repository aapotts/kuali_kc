Intellectual Property Review

The Intellectual Property Review tab enables the OSP to initiate and track the review of Background Intellectual Property (BIP) by the Technology Transfer Office (TTO).  Review of BIP is required for proposals submitted to commercial sponsors.

The Intellectual Property Review tab is read-only.  You enter and update the IP review information on the Intellectual Property Review document by clicking .
