Closeout Type

 

Use the Closeout Type maintenance document to lookup, create, and update Closeout Report Types. Closeout reports are the final reports prepared and submitted at the end of a sponsored project.  Closeout report types are added to the Awards e-doc on the Payment, Reports & Terms tab, in the Closeout panel.
