Budget Justification Panel

With a single click, gather the text from all line–item Budget Justification Notes fields.

Figure 266 Budget Justification Panel

 

Most funding agencies want justifications (explanations) for some or all line items in the budget.  Justification notes should explain when an item is needed and the benefit added to the project.  Justifications provide information for auditors, sponsor accountants, and accountants at your institution. 

Budget items without justification notes are at increased risk of modification or deletion by a sponsor who does not understand the necessity of an item. 

Budget line items normally covered under F&A should be explained (justified) on this panel.

The Budget Justification panel provides an efficient means for consolidating the individual justifications entered for cost line items.  Consolidating the notes is convenient when writing the budget justification that attaches to the proposal for review by the sponsor.  (See Notes & Attachments tab on the Proposal Development Document)

 

Budget justification notes consolidated or entered on this tab do not go to the sponsor.
