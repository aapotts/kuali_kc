Combined Credit Split Panel

The Combined Credit Split panel identifies the percentage of credit attributable to each PI & Co-I for recognition, responsibility, space, and financial credit split types.

First, you enter the credit percentage for the PI.  The PI then designates a proportion of their credit to assign to their own units.

The Combined Credit Split panel contains one row for each Key Person and each of their Units.  Credit split types are in the columns:

 

Figure 161 Combined Credit Split Panel

 

The Combined Credit Split panel displays only if your institution selects the KC Combined Credit Split option.

The KC default credit split types are Recognition, Responsibility, Space, and Financial.  Your institution may define its own credit split types.  Your institution can use up to eight types.

Your institution designates which credit split types must add to 100% during the data validation process.
