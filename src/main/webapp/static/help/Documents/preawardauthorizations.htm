Preaward Authorizations

The Preaward Authorizations panel allows you to identify both the sponsor-authorized and institutional-authorized amounts committed to the award for the preaward period, if applicable.

The Preaward Authorizations panel includes the Sponsor Authorization and Institutional Authorization sections.
