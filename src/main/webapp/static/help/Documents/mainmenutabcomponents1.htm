Main Menu Tab Components

After successful authentication and login, your user role tells KC which Main Menu tab to display.  This is your default screen.  At all times, the Menu tab you are viewing is maroon or red, as shown in Figure 33.  Most users do nearly all of their KC work on their default Main Menu tab.

Each Main Menu tab has a title indicating a general category of work and users.  The tab displays links to related tasks and functions, grouped by type of work.  The groupings are called menus.
