Notice of Opportunity

Use the Notice of Opportunity maintenance document to lookup, create, and update opportunity types.  Opportunities are the mechanisms used to communicate information about the funding opportunity for this project.  Examples include federal solicitations (e.g.  RFP, RFA, PA), nonfederal solicitations, unsolicited proposal, verbal request, etc.  You select Notice of Opportunity types on the Sponsor & Program Information panel on the KC Proposal Development e-doc’s Proposal tab.

 

To access the Notice of Opportunity Lookup screen:

Maintenance main menu tab > Shared menu group > Notice of Opportunity link
