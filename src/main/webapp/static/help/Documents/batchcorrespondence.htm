Batch Correspondence

The Batch Correspondence section of the Actions page is initially comprised of two subsections:  Generate Batch Correspondence, and Batch Correspondence History.  After a Protocol document has been submitted by the PI, IRB Administrators use this functionality to communicate with the PI and committee members during the life cycle of the protocol application.  Notifications are created, sent and tracked using the features in this section.
