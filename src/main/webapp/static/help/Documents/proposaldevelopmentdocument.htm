Proposal Development Document

Proposal Development documents are electronic documents that are part of the KC Proposal Development module and are linked to Budget documents in the system.  This section explains how to create, maintain, route and submit Proposal Development documents. 

A Proposal in KC can be developed by an individual or a group.  It provides detailed technical and business information pertaining to a proposed project that is presented to potential outside sponsor organizations, either unsolicited or in response to a specific funding announcement.  Ideas for possible projects are submitted to sponsor agencies for their review and consideration.  A successful outcome for a proposal submission is when the proposal is accepted by a sponsoring organization and an award agreement to the institution is initiated granting the researcher(s) funds to facilitate the project.

For procedural “how-to” information containing numbered task steps about initiating, copying, saving, reloading, closing, canceling, searching for, submitting and routing Proposal Development or Budget documents, see “Common E-Doc Operations” in KC Overview.
