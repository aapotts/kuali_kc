Expand/Collapse All

You may expand or collapse all tabs by clicking expand all or collapse all.  These two buttons are located at the top, right of e-doc tabs.

Clicking the collapse all button on an e-doc tab

For example, when you click the collapse all button (shown above) while one or more sections have their content displayed, all of the sections of the tab that contain the form fields become hidden from view and only the tab labels are displayed (as shown below):
