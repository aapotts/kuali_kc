Blanket Approval

Authority that is given to designate Reviewers who can approve a document to a chosen route point. The action bypasses approvals that would otherwise be required in the Routing. For an authorized Reviewer, the Doc Handler typically displays the Blanket Approval button along with the other options. Reviewers who are skipped are sent Acknowledge requests to notify them of their bypassed approval.  Authority that is given to designated Reviewers|keyword=Reviewer who can approve a document to a chosen route point.
