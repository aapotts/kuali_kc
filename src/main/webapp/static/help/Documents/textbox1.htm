Text Box

•      Click in the text box to place your cursor there.

•      Enter the text you need for this field.

•      If the box contains text that must be removed, you have four options:

1.  Place your cursor to the left of the existing text and hold down [Delete] on your keyboard to remove the text.  You may also tap the [Delete] key to remove the text.

2.  Place your cursor to the right of the existing text and hold down [Backspace] on your keyboard until the text is removed.  You may also tap the [Backspace] key to remove the text.

3.  You may drag your cursor across the text in the box to select all of it, and then tap [Delete] once to remove the text.

4.  You may select all of the text with your cursor and then begin typing your new text to replace the previous text.
