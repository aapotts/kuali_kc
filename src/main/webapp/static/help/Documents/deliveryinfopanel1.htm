Delivery Info Panel

The Delivery Info panel contains the instructions for delivery of this proposal to the sponsor:

 

The information in the Delivery panel automatically comes from the Proposal Development document or the Proposal Log document.  You may change this information here in the Institutional Proposal document. 

 

Figure 297  Delivery Info Panel

 

Table 106 Delivery Info Panel Definitions

Field

Description

Mail By

Optional

The office responsible for submitting the proposal

Click  to select a Mail By office of either OSP or Department.

Mail Account ID

Optional

The office’s Mail Account number

Enter the Mail Account ID into the box.

Mailing Name & Address

Optional

The address of the recipient of the hardcopy proposal

Click  to search for and select a recipient. 

After you add the recipient, KC displays the complete mailing address:

 

Figure 298 Mailing Address

 

Click  to remove the mailing address.

KC asks you to confirm the removal:

 

Figure 299 KC confirmation to Remove Delivery Address Info

 

Click  to remove the delivery address.

Click  to keep the delivery address and return to the Proposal tab.

Type

Optional

The method for delivering the proposal to the sponsor

Delivery types are:

•    Regular: The proposal will be mailed to the sponsor

•    Electronic: The proposal will be emailed to the sponsor

•    Delivery Service: The proposal will be delivered to the sponsor

 

Click  to select a delivery type.

Number of Copies

Optional

The number of paper copies to be mailed to the sponsor, in addition to the original

Enter the Number of Copies into the box.

Mail Description

Optional

Additional information that will assist in the delivery of the proposal

Enter a Mail Description into the box.
