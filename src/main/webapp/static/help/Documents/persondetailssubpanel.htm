Person Details Sub-Panel

The Person Details sub-panel contains the person’s contact information, home unit, and percentage effort for this proposal:

 

Figure 154 Person Details Sub-Panel

 

Your institution defines which Person Details fields you may change.

KC pre-fills some of the field on the Person Details panel according to your institution’s business rules.

Additional fields may be conditionally required for submissions to www.grants.gov.  Examples are the Contact, Title, and eRA Commons User Name fields.

 

Table 43 Person Details Sub-Panel Definitions

Proposal Person Role Id

Optional

This person’s Proposal Role

Click  to select a Proposal Role.

You can select either PI/Contact or PI/Multiple as this person’s role.

You cannot select a Proposal Person Role Id for someone who has the role of Key Person.

To change a Proposal Role to or from Key Person, you must delete the Person from the Key Personnel tab and then re-add him or her.

If a Principal Investigator or PI/Contact already exists and you change another person’s Proposal Role to Principal Investigator or PI/Contact and save, KC displays this error:

Add the person with another role or delete the current PI and add this person with the PI Role.xxx
