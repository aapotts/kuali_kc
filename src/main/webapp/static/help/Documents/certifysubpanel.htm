Certify Sub-Panel

The Certify sub-panel contains verifications and disclosure statements required prior to conducting institutional research.  Your institution and the sponsor define these. 

Each user must go to the Certify sub-panel to answer their own certification questions.  You may print the certification from the Certify sub-panel:

 

Figure 157 Certify Sub-Panel Example

 

Both the PI and the Co-I must complete the Certify sub-panel.

Your institution may or may not require Key Persons to complete the Certify sub-panel.

KC initially displays this message for Key Persons:

Click “Add Certification Question”  to display the Certification questions.
