Central Admin Menu Group

At the time of printing, the Central Admin menu tab held content identical to the Unit menu tab.  In later releases of KC, the contents of the Central Main Menu Tab will display links to activities common to central administrators.

Maintenance Menu Group

The Maintenance menu group provides access to maintenance documents (also referred to as “Reference Documents” or “Maintenance E-Docs”).  These are electronic documents that provide a user interface into reference tables holding information referenced by other e-docs in KC.

 

Table 11  Maintenance Menu Group Descriptions

Menu Group

Description

Shared

Links to maintenance documents that contain reference table information that is shared by more than one e-doc.

Miscellaneous

Certain unrelated maintenance documents that defy easy grouping into the other menu boxes on this menu screen.  While they don’t logically fit into other categories, they may still be used in more than one place within the application and by existing modules.

Proposals

Displays links to maintenance documents through which pages and panels in the Proposal document and/or Budget document are managed.

Awards

Displays links to maintenance documents through which details of awarded or sponsored proposals are stored and maintained.

Compliance – Conflict of Interest

Displays links to maintenance documents through which details of compliance activities related to COI issues are stored and maintained.

Compliance

Displays links to maintenance documents through which pages and panes regarding affiliations, committee participation, protocol, roles, and typologies related to compliance with state and federal regulations are managed.
