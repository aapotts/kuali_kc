Delivery Info Panel

The Delivery Info Panel (Figure 127) is where the PI enters delivery instructions to the OSP.

Figure 127  Delivery Info Panel

 

Table 36 Delivery Info Panel Field Definitions and Procedures

Field

Description

Mail By

A drop-down list, this field indicates whether the Office of Sponsored Programs or the department will be mailing the proposal to the agency/sponsor.  Click the down arrow to display the list and click on an item in the list to highlight and select it to populate the box with your selection.

Mail Account ID

This text field should contain an internal account number used to identify a specific sender.

Mailing Name & Address

The shipping information needed to submit hard copies of the proposal.  The name and address to which the proposal will be mailed.

Type

The mechanism used to submit the proposal (e.g.  electronic, express mail, etc.).  The method by which the proposal will be delivered to the agency/sponsor.  Click the down arrow to display the list and click on an item in the list to highlight and select it to populate the box with your selection.

Number of Copies

The number of copies to be mailed to the sponsor, in addition to the original.  The number of paper copies of the proposal that should be mailed to the agency/sponsor.

Mail Description

Any information that will assist in the delivery of the proposal.  Any information that will assist in the delivery of the proposal.

Actions

       A left click on this icon opens a popup window giving expanded text area in which to type your Mail Description.
