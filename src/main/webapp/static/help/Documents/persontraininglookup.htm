Person Training Lookup

When you click the Person Training link in the Miscellaneous menu group box on the Maintenance main menu tab, KC displays the Person Training Lookup screen:

 

Figure 589 Person Training Lookup Screen

 

Use the Person Training Lookup screen to search for and display existing Person Training Ids.

Enter appropriate information in the search fields to find the Person Training Id you need (or leave all the fields blank to display all Person Training Ids), then click  to display a list of Person Training Ids.  When you click search, KC displays the Person Training Lookup screen again, with the results of your search displayed below the buttons:

 

Figure 590 Person Training Lookup screen with search information

 

KC displays the Person Training Ids that match your search below a statement about the number of items retrieved (how many items matched your search) and the number of items displayed.

In the search results list, you can either edit or copy a Person Training Id using the links in the Actions column.

For information about modifying an existing maintenance document such as a Person Training, see Editing A Maintenance Document in Common Maintenance Document Operations.

For information about copying a maintenance document such as a Person Training, see Copying A Maintenance Document in Common Maintenance Document Operations.

The Person Training Lookup screen has a  button in the top right corner that lets you create a new Person Training Id.
