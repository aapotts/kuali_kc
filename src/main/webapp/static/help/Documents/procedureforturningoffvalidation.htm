Procedure for Turning Off Validation

You can turn off validation when you want to continue working on your proposal without receiving error messages, or when you have corrected all errors and want to submit your proposal:

 

1.  Click  to stop data validation.  KC returns the Data Validation panel to its initial display. 

 

Data Validation remains in effect until you click the turn off validation button.
