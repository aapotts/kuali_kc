Sponsor

The Sponsor sub-panel includes the Sponsor, Sponsor Award ID, Modification ID, Prime Sponsor, CFDA Number, and NSF Science Code fields.  Users with Administrator authorization may enter information in these fields.

Figure 476 Award Document, Award Page, Details & Dates Section, Sponsor Subsection Layout

 

Table 298 Award Document, Award Page, Details & Dates Section, Sponsor Subsection - Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Sponsor

Required.  The organization or agency providing support for this sponsored project.  Enter the Sponsor ID into the box.  You can confirm the sponsor ID you entered by clicking . 

If you do not know the Sponsor ID, click  to find and select one.

Sponsor Award ID

Optional.  The identification number assigned to the award document based on the sponsor’s award code.  Enter the Sponsor Award ID into the box.

Modification ID

Optional.  The change or action number assigned to the sponsor award instrument by the sponsor.  Enter the Modification ID into the box.

Prime Sponsor

Optional.  The organization or agency that is the original source of funds for the sponsored project.  Enter the Prime Sponsor into the box.  You can confirm the prime sponsor you entered by clicking .  If you do not know the Prime Sponsor, click  to find and select one.

CFDA Number

Optional.  A unique identifier for the sponsor and the funding opportunity announcement (FOA) in the Catalog for Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA).

The first 2 digits identify the Federal agency.  The last 3 digits identify the specific Federal program.

Enter the CFDA Number into the box.

NSF Science Code

Optional.  A numeric code defined by the National Science Foundation (NSF) indicating a field of research activity.  Click  to select the NSF Science Code.

 

Adding a Sponsor ID and Prime Sponsor using the Sponsor Lookup on the Sponsor Sub-Panel:

When you click  to find and select a Sponsor ID and Prime Sponsor, KC displays the Sponsor Lookup screen:

 

Figure 477 Sponsor Lookup Screen

 

Enter appropriate information in the search fields to find the Sponsor ID or Prime Sponsor you need (or leave all the fields blank to display all Sponsors), and then click  to display a list of Sponsors.  When you click search, KC displays the Sponsor Lookup screen again, with the results of your search displayed below the buttons:

 

Figure 478 Sponsor Lookup Screen with Search Information

 

KC displays the Sponsors that match your search below a statement about the number of items retrieved (how many items matched your search) and the number of items displayed.

In the search results list, click return value next to the Sponsor Code that you need, to enter the Sponsor ID or Prime Sponsor in the Sponsor sub-panel in the Details & Dates panel.
