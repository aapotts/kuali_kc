Unit Hierarchy

The Unit Hierarchy screen serves as the basis for much of the functionality found in KC.  Setting up the unit hierarchy correctly is essential to proper functioning of proposal routing and submission, applying the appropriate rates to a budget, associating awards, protocols and proposals to the correct unit, and associating users and roles to the appropriate unit(s).

The system actually groups units into a hierarchical structure so that a department is grouped with similar units under a college/school, which is then grouped with similar units under a campus.  In turn, all similar units and their children are under the parent unit (the Institution.)  In addition, the system allows each unit to set its own rates (i.e.  F&A rates, employee benefits, etc.), which trickle down to all applicable child units.
