Recipients Sub-Panel

When you click  to save the new entry in the Payment & Invoice Requirements section, KC displays a collapsed Recipients sub-panel:

 

Figure 381 Payment & Invoices Panel, Payment & Invoice Requirements Section Filled, With Collapsed Recipients Sub-Panel

 

Click  on the Recipients sub-panel to display its information:

Figure 382 Payment & Invoices Panel, Payment & Invoice Requirements Section, Recipients Sub-Panel

 

Table 143 Payment & Invoices Panel, Payment & Invoice Requirements Section, Recipients Sub-Panel Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Contact

Optional

 

The name of the contact person at the organization to which you need to send these invoices or reports for this award

Click  to select a Contact.

Name/Organization

 

Required

 

The name of the organization to which you need to send these invoices or reports

Click  to find and select a Name/Organization.

# Of Copies

Optional

 

The number of report copies you need to send to this recipient

Enter the # of Copies into the box.

Actions

Optional

 

Click  to save this recipient information.

Click  to remove the recipient on this row.
