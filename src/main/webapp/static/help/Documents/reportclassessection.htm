Report Classes Section

The Report Classes section includes these sub-panels: Financial, Property, Intellectual Property, Technical/Management, Procurement, and Proposals Due.

The following table describes the sub-panels in the Report Classes section:

 

Table 146 Reports Panel, Report Classes Section Sub-Panel Descriptions

Sub-Panel

Description

Financial

The Financial sub-panel provides a formal summary of the financial reports and/or invoices required on a sponsored project, prepared as required by the sponsor’s terms and conditions in the award.

Property

The Property sub-panel displays a formal summary of the property reports required of the institution on property purchased with project funds during the awarded project period.

Intellectual Property

The Intellectual Property (IP) sub-panel displays a formal summary of IP or patent reporting required on the award.  These reports gather information on the creations of the mind generated in the course of the scope of work of a sponsored project prepared as required by the sponsor’s terms and conditions in the award.

Technical/Management

The Technical/Management sub-panel displays a formal summary of progress and/or results reporting as required by the sponsor’s terms and conditions in the award.

Procurement

The Procurement sub-panel displays a formal summary of reports for the purchasing, leasing, renting, or selling of services or supplies as required by the sponsor’s terms and conditions in the award.  Reports include items for both purchasing and subcontracting activities.

Proposals Due

The Proposals Due sub-panel includes information on requirements for continuation proposals or other proposals required for the award.
