Key Personnel Tab

The Key Personnel tab identifies the proposal personnel and their roles on the project.  The contact information, degree, affiliated unit, and certification questions are included for each person assigned to the proposal:

 

Figure 141 Key Personnel Tab

 

Roles are defined as Principal Investigator (PI), Co-Investigators (Co-I), and Key Persons.  Requirements for completing the Key Personnel tab depend on the person’s role.  In addition, the tasks that a person receives and can do in KC Workflow depend on their role.

If your institution selects the KC Combined Credit Split option, you can use a combined credit split among Key Personnel.

KC will put the key personnel and their roles on the Grants.gov Research & Related Senior/Key Person Profile 424 form for Grants.gov submissions.

 

You may save the Key Personnel tab without adding any key personnel on it.  However, you cannot submit a proposal until you have at least a PI.
