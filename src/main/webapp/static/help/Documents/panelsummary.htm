Panel Summary

The Proposal and Personnel attachments are uploaded documents related specifically to the proposal or the personnel attached to the proposal.  Both can be viewed by the sponsor agency and all users with access.

Internal Attachments are institutional documents which include information related to the proposal, but intended for internal record keeping only (i.e.  a cost share funding agreement from your department or college).

Abstracts are concise, clear summary documents, the whole of which comprise a kind of glossary in which multiple aspects of the proposal are stored for quick reference.
