Print Reports Panel

The Print Reports panel enables you to print the Current and Pending Support reports.  The Current & Pending Support report, also called Other Support by some sponsors, lists the awards and pending proposals for a PI, Co-PI, or Key Person.  Federal sponsors often require this report with your proposal. 

The Current Support Report lists active awards and the person’s effort on those awards.  The Pending Support Report lists the person’s pending proposals and their proposed effort on those proposals. 

This information assures the sponsor that they are not funding duplicate work and that there will not be a conflict of commitment.
