Financial Panel

The Financial panel contains information about the project costs for each budget period:

 

Figure 294 Financial Panel

 

The information in the Financial panel automatically comes from the Budget document.  You may enter the information directly in the Financial panel for proposals that were not routed through the Proposal Development and Budget module.

For more information about the budget fields used on the Financial panel, see  Budget Document.
