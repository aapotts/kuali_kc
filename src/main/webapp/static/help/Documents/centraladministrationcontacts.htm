Central Administration Contacts

The Central Administration Contacts panel lists individuals with assigned roles on the project from the institution’s central administration.

 

KC automatically fills in the Central Administration Contacts panel information.  When you create an award, you must select a Lead Unit on the Award tab.  In KC 2.0, this Lead Unit fills the Central Administration Contacts (read-only) panel.  This functionality will be added to KC in a later release.
