Contacts Tab

The Contacts tab includes these panels:

•      Project Personnel

•      Unit Contacts

•      Sponsor Contacts

•      Central Administration Contacts

Each panel displays information about that type of people or contacts for this proposal.  Each panel also allows you to add and delete people of that type from this proposal.

Table 17 Contacts Tab Panel Descriptions

Panel

Description

Project Personnel

Institutional employees or non-employees assigned roles on the project

Unit Contacts

Individuals assigned roles based on their organizational unit

Sponsor Contacts

Individuals who are representatives of the sponsoring organization

Central Administration Contacts

Individuals from the institution’s central administration who have assigned roles on the project

 

Complete the same fields and steps when entering names in the Contacts panel for Project Personnel, Unit Contacts, Sponsor Contacts, and Central Administration Contacts.
