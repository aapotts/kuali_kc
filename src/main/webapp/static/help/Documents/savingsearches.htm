Saving Searches

Each time you search, KC automatically saves your search criteria with those of other users.  You may enter a name for your search to help you find and re-use it later.

 

Figure 61 Searches Dropdown List

 

Saved searches are sorted by the search name under the title –Named Searches or by the criteria you entered, such as the Date Created From field or the Document ID field.
