Date Fields

Figure 45 Example of a Date Selection Option

 

Use the mm/dd/yyyy format when you manually enter dates.  When available, you may use the Date selector (x) to enter dates.

Click the Date selector icon to display a calendar of the current month with today’s date selected.  Click the arrows on either side of the word Today to display other months or years:
