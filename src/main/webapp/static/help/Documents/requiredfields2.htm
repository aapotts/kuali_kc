Required Fields

Custom attributes can be required or optional.

Figure 166  Custom Data Page Fields May Be Required for Route/Submit, but Not Required for Save/Navigate

 

Depending on your institution’s implementation of custom data, some fields may be required to pass validation, but will never be marked as required with an asterisk.

For more information about how to configure and use Custom Data, see “KC Technical Documentation” available online at https://test.kuali.org/confluence/display/KCDOC/Home.
