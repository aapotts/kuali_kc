Figure 673 Sponsor Hierarchy Screen – After Using the Create New Button

 

Click  to create a new group in your new Sponsor Hierarchy.

Each Sponsor Hierarchy must contain at least one group before you save it.

When you click add, KC displays a popup box for the name of your new group:

 

Figure 674 Explorer User Prompt Screen for New Group Name

 

Enter a name for the new group in the box on the Explorer User Prompt screen.  Click  to save the new group or click  to delete the new group.

 

When you click OK, KC displays the Sponsor Hierarchy screen again, with this new group.

 

Figure 675 Sponsor Hierarchy Screen with a New Group Added

 

Click  to expand a Sponsor Hierarchy, group, or subgroup.

Click  to collapse a Sponsor Hierarchy, group, or subgroup.

Click  to add more groups or subgroups.  The Sponsor Hierarchy screen below shows several groups in a Sponsor Hierarchy:

 

Figure 676 Sponsor Hierarchy Screen with Several Groups

 

Click  to change the name of the group or subgroup on that line.

Use  or  to move that group or subgroup up or down in the Sponsor Hierarchy.

Click  to remove the group or subgroup on that line from this Sponsor Hierarchy.

Click  to search for and select a sponsor to add to the group or subgroup on that line.

Each Sponsor Hierarchy must contain at least one sponsor before you save it.

 

In a Sponsor Hierarchy, a group cannot contain both subgroups and sponsors at the same level.

When you click add sponsor, KC displays this screen for the group or subgroup on that line:

 

Figure 677 Sponsor Lookup Screen

 

Use the Sponsor Lookup screen to search for and select a sponsor from your list of existing sponsors. 

Within a given Sponsor Hierarchy, you can use a specific sponsor only once.

See KC User Guide Volume 2 Maintenance Documents, Sponsor for additional information on Sponsors.
