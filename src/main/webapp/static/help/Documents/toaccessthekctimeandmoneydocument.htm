You Are Here: KC TimeAndMoney Document

To Access the KC TimeAndMoney Document

Click  on the KC Award screen to access the KC TimeAndMoney document:

 

Figure 364 KC Award Screen, time & money Button

 

The KC TimeAndMoney document contains eight panels that identify management of the time and money associated with an award.  Click  on a panel to display it.

When you click  on the KC Award screen, KC displays the KC TimeAndMoney document:

 

Figure 365 KC TimeAndMoney Document

 

The Time & Money tab includes the Document Overview, Award Hierarchy, Transactions, Direct/F&A Funds Distribution, Summary, Action Summary, History, Ad Hoc Recipients, and Route Log panels that provide details about the award.

 

Table 135 KC TimeAndMoney Document panel descriptions

Panel

Description

Document Overview

See Header in the E-Doc Fundamentals section of the KC User Guide Volume 1.

Award Hierarchy

Supports the division of an Award into Units of Organization (such as budget periods or departmental allocations) defined by your institution

Transactions

Identifies the actions that add or remove money to and from the award hierarchy or move money within the award hierarchy

Direct/F&A Funds Distribution

Breakdown of funding expected for all budget periods

Summary

Overview of key identifiers and indication of award content to help users distinguish one award within the hierarchy from the others

Action Summary

Executive level summary of award transactions that affect the funding obligation

History

Overview of changes to the selected award from inception to the present

Ad Hoc Recipients

See Ad Hoc Recipients in Common Document Tabs

Route Log

Workflow actions: Pending, future, and completed
