Impersonation

Your implementation of KC may include the ability to impersonate other users after logging in.  This is particularly useful when showing how screens, documents and action options may differ between workgroups or user roles.  If the Impersonation feature is installed, you will see a text field next a login button in the upper right corner of the screen (see Figure 6).  When used, the notification area displays your ID and the one you are impersonating (see Figure 7).
