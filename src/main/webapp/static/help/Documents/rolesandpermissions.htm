Roles and Permissions

There are four roles that define a user’s permissions for a proposal.  Permissions are a set of access rights in KC:

 

Table 58 Roles and Permissions

Role

Permissions

Aggregator

 

The Aggregator may add and delete users and may modify the permissions of current users to the proposal.  

The Aggregator may modify any part of the proposal, including the proposal details, proposal narratives and attachments, and the budget.

Budget Creator

The Budget Creator may modify the proposal details and the budget.

The Budget Creator may only view the proposal narratives and attachments.

Narrative Writer

The Narrative Writer may modify the proposal details, proposal narratives, and attachments.

The Narrative Writer may only view the proposal budget.

Viewer

The Viewer may view the proposal details, the proposal narratives, attachments, and the budget.

The Viewer may not modify any part of the proposal.

 

KC assigns the Aggregator role to the user who initiates the Proposal Development document.  After saving the Required Fields for Saving Document panel, the Aggregator goes to the Permissions tab to add, delete, or modify users and their roles.

Only users with the Aggregator role may add, modify, and delete users and roles on the Permissions tab.  Users with roles other than Aggregator may only view users and their roles.
