Sponsor Funding Transferred

The Sponsor Funding Transferred sub-panel identifies funding awarded by the sponsoring agency but received from the budget of another federal agency via inter-agency transfer.

When OSP (Office of Sponsored Projects) knows that a federal award includes funding transferred from another federal entity (with a different sponsor code), OSP identifies the other entity using this sub-panel.

Users with Administrator authorization may enter information in this field.
