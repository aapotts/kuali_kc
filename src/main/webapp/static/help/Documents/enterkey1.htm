Enter Key

On all screens in KC, if you do not have abutton or link highlighted for selection, pressing your keyboard’s Enter key refreshes the screen.  If you highlight a button or link, when you press the Enter key, you activate the button or link instead of refreshing the screen. 

Your institution’s implementation of KC may provide other behaviors for the Enter key on a tab-by-tab or section-by-section basis.
