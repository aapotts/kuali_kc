Tab Sequence

Many KC screens allow you to use tab navigation.  When this feature is present, you may use the Tab key to move from one field to the next on the screen.

On all screens, the default tabbing sequence is top-to-bottom (down the first column, then on to the second column and down it, and so on) unless otherwise specified by instructions appearing on the relevant portion of the screen. 

KC sometimes provides tabbing that is left-to-right (across the first row, across the second row, and so on).

When you use the tab key to move around a page, the cursor first moves through the editable fields, then it goes through the action buttons that do not take you to another screen. 

In cases where the tabbing sequence does not strictly follow a top-to-bottom or left-to-right pattern, the fields still come first and the action buttons are accessed after the fields.
