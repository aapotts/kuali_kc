Ynq Explanations Panel

Your institution may provide additional information about the proposal on the Ynq Explanations sub-panel.  This additional information may include links to policy and procedure information and links to external sites.

The type of Ynq explanation

Your institution defines the Ynq explanation types.  Some common Ynq explanation types are Explanation, Policy, and Regulation.

Additional information about the question

Your institution provides additional information about questions.  This can be additional text to further describe a question.  It can include links to policies, procedures, and regulatory information.
