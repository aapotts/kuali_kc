Sponsor Sub-Panel

The Sponsor sub-panel includes the Sponsor, Sponsor Award ID, Modification ID, Prime Sponsor, CFDA Number, and NSF Science Code fields.  Users with Administrator authorization may enter information in these fields.

 

Figure 337 KC Award, Dates & Details Panel, Sponsor Sub-Panel

 

Table 124 KC Award, Dates & Details Panel, Sponsor Sub-Panel Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Sponsor

Required

 

The organization or agency providing support for this sponsored project

 

Enter the Sponsor ID into the box.  You can confirm the sponsor ID you entered by clicking .

 

If you do not know the Sponsor ID, click  to find and select one.

Sponsor Award ID

Optional

 

The identification number assigned to the award document based on the sponsor’s award code

 

Enter the Sponsor Award ID into the box.

Modification ID

Optional

 

The change or action number assigned to the sponsor award instrument by the sponsor

 

Enter the Modification ID into the box.

Prime Sponsor

Optional

 

The organization or agency that is the original source of funds for the sponsored project

 

Enter the Prime Sponsor into the box.  You can confirm the prime sponsor you entered by clicking .

 

If you do not know the Prime Sponsor, click  to find and select one.

CFDA Number

Optional

 

A unique identifier for the sponsor and the funding opportunity announcement (FOA) in the Catalog for Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA)

The first 2 digits identify the Federal agency.  The last 3 digits identify the specific Federal program.

Enter the CFDA Number into the box.

NSF Science Code

Optional

 

A numeric code defined by the National Science Foundation (NSF) indicating a field of research activity

 

Click  to select the NSF Science Code.
