Rates Tab

The Rates tab of the Budget Document displays negotiated Federal Institute Rates that affect your proposal.  The panels displayed on this tab identify logical groups of rates.  For each rate you may elect to accept the default rate (the negotiated Federal rate for your institution) or override the rate, line item by line item.  Override rates affect only the budget version identified in the document header.

Figure 220 Panels on the Rates Tab of the Budget Document

 

The panels shown in Figure 220 are the default panels for the Rates tab.  Your institution may install other panels.
