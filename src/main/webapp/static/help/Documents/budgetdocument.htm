Budget Document

Budget Documents are electronic documents (e-docs) attached to a specific Proposal Development Document in KC.  A budget is an estimate of costs associated with the performance of a proposed research project.  The Budget Document in KC allows you to create multiple budget versions in which you may specify performance periods, personnel, and costs while taking advantage of automated calculations for inflation, salary increases, and overhead. 

The Workflow system in KC can route (circulate) e-docs for review and approval. 

KC stores Budget Documents for reference and modification. 

A Budget Document cannot exist apart from a Proposal Development Document.
