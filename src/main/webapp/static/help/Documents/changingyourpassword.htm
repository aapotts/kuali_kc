Changing Your Password

 

On the Researcher tab, find the the Quicklinks menu.

1.  Click Change Password.

2.  The Change Password panel appears:

 

Figure 11 Change Password Panel and Save Button

 

3.  Populate the three fields as directed.  * Asterisks appear in place of the characters you type

4.  Click save
