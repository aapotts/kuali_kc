Proposal Questions Panel: Example

The Proposal Questions panel may contain any number of questions.  Your institution creates and manages these questions.  The screen print and definitions below are examples of some commonly used proposal questions and answer techniques.

You must answer all of the questions on this panel.  You may choose answers of Yes, No, and sometimes N/A (not applicable).  Some questions require secondary answers based on the first answer to the question.  If secondary answers are required, KC displays information about the requirement under the question text:
