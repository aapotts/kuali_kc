Commitments Tab

The Commitments tab contains three panels that identify financial commitments for the award.  These may come from sources other than the sponsor.

When you click the Commitments tab, KC displays this screen:

 

Figure 353 Commitments Tab Panels

 

The Commitments tab includes Cost Sharing, Rates, and Preaward Authorizations panels that provide details about the award.

 

Table 129 Commitments Tab Panel Descriptions

Panel

Description

Cost Sharing

The part of total project costs paid from sources other than the sponsor, as committed in the proposal budget

This panel displays percentage of the total award, date, and amount.

Rates

F&A and Benefits rates applicable to the award

Preaward Authorizations

Both sponsor-authorized and institutional-authorized amounts committed to the award for the pre-award period, if applicable
