Panels: Expanded and Collapsed

Panels are divisions on a tab.  Panels display fields, read-only data, dropdown menus, radio buttons, and a host of other links and functions.  Panels may have:

•      Sub-panels

•      Logical sections with headings

•      Sub-panels in the sections

You may expand a panel to display all of its information.  You may also collapse a panel so that KC displays only that panel’s title.

•      Click  to collapse a panel and hide all of its contents.

•      Click  to expand a panel and display its contents.
