Procedure for Removing an Opportunity from the Proposal

You may remove a www.grants.gov opportunity from a proposal.

 

1.  Click remove opportunity

 

         

     Figure 135 Remove Opportunity Button

 

          KC displays a confirmation message:

 

           

     Figure 136 Remove Opportunity Confirmation Message

 

2.  Click  to remove this opportunity from this proposal.  Click  to keep the opportunity.

 

 

KC removes the remove opportunity button after you remove the opportunity from this proposal.

KC no longer displays this opportunity on sub-panels after you remove it.

KC clears this Opportunity ID, Opportunity Title, and its CFDA Number in the Sponsor & Program Information panel on the Proposal tab after you remove it.
